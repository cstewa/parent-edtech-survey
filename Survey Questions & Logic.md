## Section 4: Policy Preferences

**Q11. Which of the following requests would you support?** *(Required, check all that apply)*

Each option has a bold title and a description underneath:

- **Reconsider the 1:1 device model and return to shared devices in K-8**
  Replace 1:1 devices with shared options (e.g., computer carts or labs) through elementary and middle school

- **Keep school devices at school in elementary, limit take-home in middle school**
  Devices always stay at school in elementary; sent home only when needed for specific assignments in middle school

- **Paper-and-pencil homework only for elementary school**
  School-issued devices remain at school; homework does not require screens

- **Prohibit recreational screen use during the school day**
  No Chromebook/iPad use before school, during lunch, recess, or downtime

- **Require explicit opt-in for generative AI tools**
  Parental consent required before students use AI tools, with clear disclosure of purpose and data use

- **Guarantee right to opt out of unproven EdTech and AI**
  A clear, penalty-free pathway for families to opt out of educational technology and AI tools that have not been proven safe or effective for student learning

- **Parental consent for each data-collecting app or website**
  Individual consent required for each app/site that collects student data

- **Parental access to student device activity**
  Access to information about daily screen time and which apps/websites were accessed on school-issued devices

- **Invest in teacher training and resources for non-digital instruction**
  Ensure teachers are trained and supported to offer paper-based, hands-on, and collaborative alternatives to screen-based assignments

- **Other**
