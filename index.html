Parent/Caregiver Survey

Welcome!

This survey is designed to understand how parents and caregivers across Pennsylvania feel about technology and screen time in schools.

We know every community is different — what works in one district may not work in another, and families have a wide range of experiences and perspectives. We want to hear from all of them.

Your responses will help us build a public dashboard where you can explore and compare feedback across school districts throughout the state. The goal is to give families, educators, and policymakers a clearer picture of how communities are thinking about these important issues.

The survey takes about 5 minutes. Thank you for sharing your voice.
